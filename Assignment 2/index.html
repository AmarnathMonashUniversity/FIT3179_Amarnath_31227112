Student Migration From Around The World And Entering Into Australia

International student migration into Australia from 2002 to 2021, a visualisation by Amarnath Ilangovan

Do you know that the international students in Australia occupy almost 40% of the student population. In that, around 23.6% of students are currently studying on-campus. This percentage figure is almost 10% lower when compared to the pre-coronavirus pandemic period. Particularly, in this visualisation you will be able to analyse the trend in student migration from various parts of the world and entering Australia to pursue their higher studies.

Furthermore, you will be able to witness the impacts of COVID-19 pandemic on student migration and which are the major countries that are being affected and compare the student influx into Australia.
